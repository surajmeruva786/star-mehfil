Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through the Stars

Star Mehfil was founded in 2010 with a singular mission: to bring the authentic flavors of Hyderabad to food enthusiasts. Our journey began when our founder, Chef Rahim Khan, decided to share his family's centuries-old biryani recipe with the world.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to authenticity. We use traditional cooking methods like the 'Dum' technique, where the biryani is slow-cooked in sealed containers to lock in all the flavors and aromas.

Our celestial-themed ambiance complements our stellar cuisine, creating an unforgettable dining experience that transports you to the royal courts of the Nizams.
